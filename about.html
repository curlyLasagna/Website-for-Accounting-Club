Ever since the first freshman class of 2002, the Gulf Park Campus was regarded as the “secret weapon” of The University of Southern Mississippi. The Gulf Park Accounting Society main goal is to realize the potential of the coast by collaborating with business to expose accounting students to their potential employers. Another goal is to bring students together and improve the overall social community of the campus.
